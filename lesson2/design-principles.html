PARC: Proximity

Wikipedia

wikipedia.org

Wikipedia has a good example of proximity on their landing page. While it's mostly words and has potential to be confusing, the user can see what belongs together based only on proximity. At the bottom of the page, the same proximity is achieved with icons.

PARC: Alignment

Deku Deals

dekudeals.com

Deku Deals effectively demonstrates alignment by having each picture perfectly in line with the others. There are two identical columns and each picture aligns with the picture below and and to the side.

PARC: Repetition

Apple

apple.com

There aren't many companies more well known than Apple for repetition with colors and branding. The stark, clean white that Apple is known for is front and center on their site. There is repetition with the prevalence of the Apple logo and the 'i' naming convention.
