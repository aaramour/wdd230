Persona 1

Name

Karen, the Mother of Her Children

Goals

To expose her children to the world while protecting them from it, to socialize, and to find a deal

Site Use

Find local activities for her family, find social locations, and find places to buy things

Persona 2

name

Gary, the Small Business Owner

Goals

Grow his business, connect with more consumers, and earn more money

Site Use

Find events, consumer locations, and advertising to achieve his goals

Scenarios

1. Karen is looking for something to do with her kids on a specific weekend coming up in Yankeetown. This site will need a calendar for her to easily check.

2. Karen wants to know what activities there will be at a local festival. There will need to be a list of businesses and activities.

3. Gary wants to advertise for his local business. The site needs to have a place where businesses can advertise.

4. Gary wants to partner with another local business so they can benefit each other. The site should have a listing of business affiliates.

5. Gary wants to find locations where a lot of consumers will be. The site needs a page of upcoming events.
