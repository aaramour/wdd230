Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to benefit the community. It is a place for small businesses to visit in order to find/sponsor/create events that will have customers. Small businesses will also find other businesses they are able to partner with to grow each other. The members of the community will find/create/sponsor events to attend to bring members of the community together for the benefit of being social and creating fellowship, all the while supporting local businesses. The ultimate purpose, therefore, is to bring members of the community together, whether business owners or patrons, to grow Yankeetown.
